Datasets employed in [1]

The dataset employed in our work [1] is available at the servers in Universidade Federal de Ouro Preto (UFOP) through the following link.

http://www.decom.ufop.br/menotti/imgs-vlpl

Through this link, there are available two packages:

imgs-UFOP-gate.rar

Contains the Brazilian license plates we acquired at UFOP campus.

imgs-greek-vehicles.rar

Contains the Greek license plates originally made available by Anagnostopoulos et al. [2]. The original version of the dataset is available in their website. The version we make available herein contains extra information regarding the boundaries of each license plate located in the images. If you use those data, please, consider citing the work of Anagnostopoulos et al. [2].

If this dataset is employed in the development of your research, please, make sure our work [1] is cited. BibTeX entry is available at the bottom of this page.
