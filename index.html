Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Computing (IC) of University of Campinas (UNICAMP).

For academic background, consider the following. For a great part of 2019, I have been in a short-time postdoctoral research at Politecnico di Milano (Polimi) in Italy investigating forensic problems in the context of open-set scenarios [1] [7]. I have obtained the master’s degree [14] as well as doctor’s degree [13] through UNICAMP in Brazil investigating recognition in open-set scenarios [4] [12]. As extra works, I have some collaborations—also in machine learning—in medical problems [5] [6]. Through Federal University of Ouro Preto (UFOP) I had obtained the bachelor’s degree in Computer Science [15]. During the bachelors, I had worked mainly with digital image processing in the speciﬁc problem of vehicle license plate location [10] and I concluded my education studying the Haskell functional programming language for image processing [15].
