I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Computing (IC) of University of Campinas (UNICAMP) [1, 2]. For academic background, consider the following. For a great part of 2019, I have been in a short-time postdoctoral research at Politecnico di Milano (Polimi) in Italy investigating forensic problems in the context of open-set scenarios [3, 9]. I have obtained the M.Sc. degree [15] as well as Ph.D. degree [14] through UNICAMP in Brazil investigating recognition in open-set scenarios [6, 2]. As extra works, I have some collaborations—also in machine learning—in medical problems [7, 8]. Through Federal University of Ouro Preto (UFOP) I had obtained the bachelor’s degree in Computer Science [16]. During the bachelors, I had worked mainly with digital image processing in the speciﬁc problem of vehicle license plate location [12] and I concluded my education studying the Haskell functional programming language for image processing [16].
