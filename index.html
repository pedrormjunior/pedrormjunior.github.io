pedrormjunior@gmail.com

I work mainly with recognition in open-set scenarios, investigating methods that can be applied to general open-set problems [3] [10]. I have obtained the master’s degree [12] as well as doctor’s degree [11] through University of Campinas (UNICAMP) in open-set recognition. I have some collaborations in machine learning works for medical problems [4] [5]. Through Federal University of Ouro Preto (UFOP) I had obtained the bachelor’s degree in Computer Science [13]. During the undergraduate, I had worked mainly with digital image processing in the speciﬁc problem of vehicle license plate location [8] and I concluded my education studying the Haskell functional programming language for image processing [13].
