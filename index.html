I work as a researcher at Artificial Intelligence Lab. Recod.ai of the Instituto de Computação (IC) at Universidade Estadual de Campinas (UNICAMP) since mid-2024, previously as a postdoctoral researcher [1, 2, 4, 5, 12, 13, 14, 15].

For a great part of 2019, I have been in a short-time postdoctoral research at Politecnico di Milano (Polimi) in Italy investigating forensic problems in the context of open-set scenarios [6, 16].

I have obtained the M.Sc. [22] as well as Ph.D. degree [21] through UNICAMP in Brazil — with a 1-year internship at University of Colorado Colorado Springs (UCCS) — investigating recognition in open-set scenarios [3, 7, 9, 24, 25].

Through Universidade Federal de Ouro Preto (UFOP) I had obtained the bachelor’s degree in Computer Science [23]. During the bachelors, I had worked mainly with digital image processing in the specific problem of vehicle license plate location [19, 20] and I concluded my education studying the Haskell functional programming language for image processing [23].

As extra work, I have some collaborations — also in machine learning — in medical problems [10, 11, 26], estimation of geolocation through images [17, 18], and on the development of a framework for machine learning experiments [8].
